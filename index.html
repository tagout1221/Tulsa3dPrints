What We Offer

Our Services

FDM Printing

Durable functional parts in PLA, PETG, TPU, PLA-CF, and PETG-CF. Ideal for prototypes, enclosures, brackets, and end-use components.

Multi-Color Printing

Up to 4 colors or materials in a single print via our AMS system. Perfect for color-matched parts, gradients, logos, and multi-material assemblies.

Rapid Prototyping

Fast-turnaround prototypes to test, validate, and iterate your designs before committing to expensive tooling or mass production runs.

Custom Parts

One-off and small-batch custom mechanical parts, housings, brackets, and assemblies. Just send us your STL file and we'll handle the rest.

Rush Orders

Need it by tomorrow? Our 24-hour rush service keeps your project on track. Ideal for last-minute deadlines and time-sensitive prototypes.
